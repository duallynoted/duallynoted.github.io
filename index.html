Biography

I am a software developer who specializes in front-to-back, full stack programming in the JavaScript programming language. As a full stack developer, I am responsible for writing, testing, and debugging programming code for multiple JavaScript frameworks such as React, AngularJS, and jQuery; as well as backend technologies such as Node, Express, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB. I am involved in various aspects of product development, with a particular interest in customer relations. I have created several applications which utilize the latest technology to solve problems. Prior to delving into software engineering, I was a college administrator and Veterans Benefits Coordinator. My inclination for solving problems, along with my military experience and background in higher education, has helped me develop my prowess for anticipating and interpreting what clients need. I graduated from Tennessee Technological University with a B.S. in Secondary Education, took courses at Northern Kentucky University toward a Masters of Public Administration, and later this month, I will be graduating from Prime Digital Academy with a Full Stack Software Engineering Certificate
